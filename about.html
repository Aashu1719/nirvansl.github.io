About Us

Our Mission

1. Nirvan Roopam Modern Sr. Secondary schools value discipline among its students , most evident by their motto of service before self .The school aims to preserve and enhance the highest standards of excellence and prepare generations for the 21st century . The students of Nirvan Roopam Modern Sr. Secondary schools help to master the expanding horizons of technology and also become the repository of a deep commitment to Indians ethos and culture in its pristine form. Nirvan Roopam Modern Sr. Secondary school students are equipped to access the best opportunity of growth and advancement by being and being prepared linguistically and culturally for them. The students are carefully sensitized to environment concerns, the feeling of nationalism and communal harmony. They very well understand the contemporary challenges of education and commit to fulfilling collective responsibilities towards children in the country.
2. The schools are known for their importance given to all walks of life from academics to sports to cultural activities. In every academic session, Nirvana Roopam Modern Sr. Secondary school hosts many competitions like art, dance, music, quiz, science spelling along with all sports and much more all through the year.
3. The school allows only English as the medium of communication which is practiced vigorously too. Through this, communication skills are addressed and given special importance. The classroom is equipped with a smart system like education which helps in the student’s visual understanding. Teachers are also provided a laptop to help personalize their teaching methods, so that no student is left out. Apart from such facilities, the schools actively promote students to participate in National and international Olympiads. The school provides scholarships to encourage upcoming students in every field.
4. The success of our school can be attributed to our ability to continually identify and respond to the changing demands across the dynamic education sector as well evidenced in the prestigious seals of excellence received by us over the past many years. Our success lies with our students as well as they continue to enhance our reputation and make a positive impact on today's global demands. Our very committed parent body also upholds our common deals and aspirations as we create a challenging and enriching educational experience for our students. .

History

1. Nirvan Roopam Modern Sr. Secondary school ranks high among the premier progressive school in the country. Nirvan Roopam Modern society’s true parent body is a registered society founded in 2006. The school spread over one acre of prime land. It is today one of the most distinguished members of Nirvana Roopam modern society and has played a pivotal role. Though progressive in its outlook. Nirvana Roopam Modern Sr. Secondary school has its moorings in our strong cultural footage and traditions.
2. Smt. Usha Gandhi who is also the chairman of the school also upholds the same ideology. Her motivation, seal, and commitment cascade down to the staff and the students, who under the able leadership of principal Mr. Virat Rajput continuously strive to achieve excellence in all fields.
3. Nirvan Roopam Modern Sr. Secondary school is affiliated with the Central Board Of Secondary Education.

Our Vision & Our Motto

1. To Strive for better and better, not resting on one's laurels; Seek solutions not excuses, deliver results and some the community by providing quality education.
2. Today is a world where society is increasingly fragmented and polarized. Our school remains a community where honesty and integrity matter, sincerity and hard work are equally important values, and where the process of education is as important as the grades.
3. To develop scholastic personalities and impart knowledge to maximize skills and competencies.
4. To make students responsible, law abiding and enlightened citizens of a global society.
5. “A building with your walks and tomorrow inside”, Our School is a Community of lifelong learners, responsible global citizens and champions of our own success and a community with high expectation and high academic achievement.
